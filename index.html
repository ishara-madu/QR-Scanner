Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in the QR & Barcode Scanner mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Camera Access: We require camera permissions to scan QR codes and barcodes. The scanning process happens locally on your device. We do not upload your images or video feed to any servers.

Vibration: The app uses the device's vibration motor to provide haptic feedback upon a successful scan.

Internet Access: Internet permission is used exclusively for loading advertisements and allowing the user to open scanned URLs in their browser.

2. Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data such as your device's advertising ID to personalize ads. You can learn more about how Google uses data by visiting: Google Advertising Policy.

3. Data Storage

Any scan history or saved data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a database of your scans on our servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
